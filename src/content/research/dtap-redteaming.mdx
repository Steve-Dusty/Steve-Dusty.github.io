Working on agentic red-teaming for frontier LLMs on top of [DTAP (DecodingTrust-Agent Platform)](https://decodingtrust-agent.com/), a controllable simulation platform for AI agent red-teaming across 50+ realistic environments.

## Contribution

Generated 500+ adversarial datasets across 10 risk categories, achieving >50% ASR (attack success rate) against Opus 4.7, Llama, and DeepSeek models.

The work focuses on the gap between what red-team benchmarks measure and what actually fails when an agent is dropped into a real environment with tools and state.
